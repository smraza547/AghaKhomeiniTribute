Time Line of Imam Khomeini:

  • 1902 - Born in Khomeyn, Iran

  • 1962 - Khomeini organizes protest to allow women to vote, which is passed and then overruled.

  • 1963 - Martial Law imposed by Shah and arrests Ayatollah

  • 1964 - Khomeini is released from custody and then exiled for 15 years.

  • 1965 - Khomeini leaves Turkey for Najaf, Iraq (where he will spend 15 years)

  • 1970 - Ayatollah gives lectures which are compiled in a book(with a deceiving title) and smuggled into Iran for his supporters

  • 1977 - Son of Ayatollah dies

  • 1978 - Ayatollah is accused of impractices and Ayatollah is forced to move to Paris by the country's neighbours.

  • 1979 - Revolutionary Council formed by Ayatollah and retirns to Iran after exile.

  • 1980 - University Jihad decree is passed by Ayatollah

  • 1981 - Ali Khamenei officially became president.

  • 1989 - Died in Tehran, Iran
